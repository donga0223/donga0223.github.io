I have been working as a Postdoctoral Researcher in [Reich Lab](https://reichlab.io) within the Department of Epidemiology and Biostatistics at UMass, Amherst since 2022. I received my Ph.D. in the department of Mathematics and Statistics from the University of Massachusetts in 2022 under the supervision of [Krista J. Gile](https://people.math.umass.edu/~gile/). During ph.D. I worked to develop statistical methodology for network sampling (Respondent Driven Sampling), dependent data, nonparametric test statistics, and Bayesian inference. Now I'm working to develop statistical methods for real-time infectious disease forecasting.
